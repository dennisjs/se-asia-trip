Trip Itinerary

June 1 – June 5

Bangkok arrival & exploration
Grand Palace, Wat Pho, Wat Arun
Explore Chinatown, Chatuchak Market (weekend)
Day trip to Ayutthaya (ancient capital ruins)
Khao San Road / Sukhumvit for nightlife

June 5 – June 8

Chiang Mai (fly from Bangkok)
Old City temples: Wat Phra Singh, Wat Chedi Luang
Doi Suthep mountain temple
Night Bazaar & Nimmanhaemin Road cafés
Elephant Nature Park (ethical sanctuary day trip)

June 8 – June 12

Pai (3-hour scenic drive from Chiang Mai)
Pai Canyon, hot springs, bamboo bridge
Waterfalls: Mo Paeng & Pam Bok
Chill cafés, walking street night market
Rent a scooter to explore countryside

June 12 – June 16

Chiang Rai
White Temple (Wat Rong Khun), Blue Temple (Wat Rong Suea Ten)
Black House Museum (Baan Dam)
Golden Triangle (Mekong river trip to Laos border area)
Night Bazaar & Clock Tower light show

June 16 – June 20

Enter Laos (via Chiang Khong / Huay Xai border)
2-day Mekong Slow Boat to Luang Prabang (beautiful journey)

June 20 – June 27

Luang Prabang
Kuang Si waterfalls
Mount Phousi sunset hike
Alms giving ceremony at dawn
Night market and riverside cafés
Optional: day trip to Pak Ou Caves

June 27 – July 3

Vang Vieng (4-5 hrs bus from Luang Prabang)
Tubing / kayaking Nam Song River
Blue Lagoon swimming
Hot air balloon ride at sunset
Scenic karst landscape hikes

July 3 – July 8

Vientiane (capital of Laos)
Pha That Luang (golden stupa)
Buddha Park
Mekong riverside night market
Café culture & massage spas

July 8 – July 18

Hanoi & Northern Vietnam (fly or bus/train from Vientiane)
Hanoi Old Quarter, Hoan Kiem Lake
Halong Bay cruise (2 days / 1 night)
Ninh Binh (Tam Coc boat ride & Mua Cave hike)
Sapa (rice terraces & hill tribe trekking, 3-4 days)

July 18 – July 28

Central Vietnam (train or fly to Da Nang/Hue)
Hue Imperial City & royal tombs
Hoi An Ancient Town, lantern-lit nights
My Son Sanctuary (Cham ruins)
Da Nang beaches & Marble Mountains

July 28 – August 8

Southern Vietnam
Ho Chi Minh City (War Remnants Museum, Cu Chi Tunnels)
Mekong Delta (floating markets in Can Tho)
Optional beach trip: Phu Quoc Island or Vung Tau

August 8 – August 18

Phnom Penh, Cambodia (bus or flight from Ho Chi Minh City)
Royal Palace & Silver Pagoda
Killing Fields & Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum
Riverside promenade & night market

August 18 – August 28

Siem Reap & Angkor Wat
Explore Angkor Wat (sunrise), Ta Prohm, Bayon
Floating villages on Tonle Sap Lake
Pub Street & markets

August 28 – September 2

Return to Bangkok (overland or flight from Siem Reap)
Final shopping, rooftop bars
Maybe a side trip to Kanchanaburi (Erawan waterfalls & Bridge on River Kwai) before flying home
