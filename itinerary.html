Trip Itinerary

June 1: Arrive in Bangkok

Land at Suvarnabhumi Airport. Settle into Solitaire Bangkok Hotel.

June 5: Fly to Chiang Mai

Explore temples, markets, and local food. Stay near the old city.

June 8: Bus to Pai

Enjoy scenic roads, hot springs, and relaxed vibes in Pai.

June 12: Bus to Chiang Rai

Visit the White Temple and explore the quiet city.

June 15: Cross into Laos

Take slow boat on Mekong to Luang Prabang. Explore waterfalls and night markets.

June 22: Vientiane

Relax in the capital city, visit temples and riverside cafes.

June 25: Cross into Vietnam

Head to Hanoi, eat street food, and visit the old quarter.

July 1–15: Vietnam (north to south)

Visit Halong Bay, Hue, Hoi An, and Ho Chi Minh City.

July 20: Enter Cambodia

Explore Angkor Wat, Siem Reap, and Phnom Penh.

August 10: Return to Thailand

Fly back to Bangkok for final days of relaxing, shopping, and dining.

September 2: Fly Home

Depart from Bangkok back to the US.
